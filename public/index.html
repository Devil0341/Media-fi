MEDIA-fi

Music to match your mood.

MEDIA-fi will help you get out of your playlist funk. Pick a gif that represents how you're currently feeling and let MEDIA-fi recommend a list of relevant songs. Then, follow any song link to sample your matches on Spotify's web player and discover new music.

MEDIA-fi is created by Jonathan Drabot, Greg Petropolous, and Anisha McDowell using the Spotify API and Giphy API.
